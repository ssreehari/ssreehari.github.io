## How to Add Photos

To populate this gallery:

1. **Save your photos** in `/assets/images/photography/`
2. **Edit this file** (`photography.md`)
3. **Uncomment the example structure** above and duplicate for each photo
4. **Replace** `photo1.jpg` with your filename
5. **Add descriptions** in the `alt` attribute

The gallery uses a responsive grid that automatically adjusts to screen size. Photos will display with hover effects.

### Recommended Image Specs

- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Size**: 1200-2000px on longest side
- **File size**: Under 500KB each (compress if needed)
- **Aspect ratio**: Any (grid adjusts automatically)
