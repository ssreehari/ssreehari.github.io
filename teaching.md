## Teaching Philosophy

I believe in teaching through storytelling and building mathematical intuition before diving into formalism. My approach emphasizes:
- Connecting abstract concepts to real-world applications
- Developing computational thinking alongside theoretical understanding
- Encouraging hands-on experimentation with code and data
- Building fluency through worked examples and progressive complexity
