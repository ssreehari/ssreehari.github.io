## Join My Group

I am actively recruiting motivated PhD students, postdoctoral researchers, and undergraduate students to join my research group at the Medical College of Wisconsin.

### What I Look For

I value students and researchers who:
- Can bridge theory and implementation
- Write clear, well-documented code
- Communicate technical ideas effectively
- Are curious about both mathematical foundations and real-world applications
- Work collaboratively and support their peers

### Research Areas

Current and planned research projects include:
- **Computational Imaging**: MRI reconstruction, super-resolution, inverse problems
- **Bayesian Machine Learning**: Plug-and-Play methods, Consensus Equilibrium
- **Explainable AI**: Information geometry, latent space analysis
- **Medical Image Analysis**: Biomedical informatics, multimodal data fusion
- **Privacy-Preserving ML**: Secure transforms, federated learning

### How to Apply

If you're interested in joining my group at MCW, please email me at **ssreehari [at] mcw [dot] edu** with:

1. **Your CV and transcript**
2. **Brief research statement** (1 page):
   - What research areas interest you and why
   - Relevant coursework or projects
   - Your career goals
3. **Any publications, preprints, or code repositories** (if applicable)

For PhD applicants, please also apply through the [MCW Graduate School](https://www.mcw.edu/education/graduate-school) and mention my name in your application.
